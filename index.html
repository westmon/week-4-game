You will be given a random number at the start of the game. The objective is to match your total score with the random number that is displayed. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Make sure to not surpass the random number, or you'll lose. Each time the game starts, the value of the random number, and the value of the crystals, changes.
